Servo Gripper Module

SKU: TR-SGM-01

$22.00

A parallel-jaw end-effector driven by a standard SG90 or MG996R servo. The rack-and-pinion linkage converts servo rotation to linear jaw travel with minimal backlash. Both jaws move symmetrically so your grasp center stays fixed regardless of object width.

Finger inserts are swappable — flat TPU pads for fragile objects, V-groove PLA inserts for round stock. Mounts to any Titan actuator output flange via four M3 bolts on a 20mm BCD pattern. Compatible with any servo outputting 4.8–6V.
