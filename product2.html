Dual-Stage Arm Actuator

SKU: TR-DSA-48

$64.00

Two stacked cycloidal stages give this actuator a 441:1 overall reduction — enough holding torque to lock a full robotic arm segment under load without an external brake. The intermediate shaft runs on dual flanged bearings to handle both axial and radial loads from a cantilevered arm link.

Designed around a 48mm outer frame with integrated mounting flanges compatible with 20mm aluminum extrusion. The output face accepts any standard 5mm keyed shaft attachment, and the input face bolts directly to NEMA 17 or NEMA 23 steppers.
